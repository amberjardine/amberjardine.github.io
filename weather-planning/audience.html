Target Audience

The target audience for this site is anyone who spends time outdoors regularly. The audience of the site will use the site to find out when there will be a good sunny day. This audience could include beach-goers, hikers, families who want to go camping, etc.

Kaylee

Age: 19
Occupation: Student
Hobbies: surfing, biking, gymnastics, hiking, reading, dancing.
Kaylee uses this site to find out what day is best to go outside. Kaylee practically lives outdoors, so knowing what the weather is going to be like is very important to her. When planning her week, Kaylee uses the website to figure out what day is the best to go surfing, which day to go biking, and which days to stay inside.

Isaac

Age: 25
Occupation: Rock Climbing Instructor
Hobbies: rock climbing, hiking, mountain biking, watching movies.
Isaac owns a rock climbing business. His business really relies on good weather, so Isaac uses this website very often when booking customers to go rock climbing. Isaac is a very outdoorsy guy. When he isn't working, he is usually hiking or mountain biking. On cold or rainy days, Isaac likes to stay home and watch movies or play video games.

Scenarios

  1. What is the weather forecast for the next ten days?
  2. When is it going to be sunny next?
  3. What are the high and low temperatures going to be over the next ten days?
  4. What day would be best to spend time outside?
  5. What day will it be both sunny and warm enough for a day oudoors?
